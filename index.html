Let's Dance!

Dance Instruction Services

We provide lessons for all kinds of ballroom dances including: foxtrot, waltz, tango, swing, cha-cha and much more. Singles and couples welcome. No contracts! In addition to the lessons listed below, we also host dance parties a few times a year as well as performance parties twice a year where our students showcase their hard work!

Group Lessons

Come by yourself or bring a friend! Group lessons will focus on a single dance style for the hour. The cost is only $10 per person! Be sure to follow us on Facebook for our calendar and posts about group lessons.

Private Lessons

Lessons can be individual or for a couple. You can choose the dance style of focus or let our talented instructors choose for you. The cost is only $40 per lesson.

Wedding Choreography

Work with our amazing instructors to choreograph the wedding dance of your dreams! Each lesson is $40.

Our Amazing Instructors

Lead Instructor
